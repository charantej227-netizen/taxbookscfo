Our Vision

To be the most trusted financial partner for businesses and practitioners across America.

We believe world-class accounting, CFO guidance and transaction advisory should not be exclusive to corporations that can afford Big 4 fees. TaxBooks.CFO exists to bridge that gap — delivering institutional-grade expertise at pricing that makes sense for growth-stage businesses and independent professionals.

  • Premium quality — without the premium-only price tag
  • One relationship covering tax, finance and transactions
  • An organisation built with the depth to grow alongside your business
Our Mission

To be the one-stop financial partner every business deserves — but rarely finds.

Most businesses fragment their advisory relationships — a CPA for tax, a separate bookkeeper, and a referral for transactions. That fragmentation costs time, money and continuity. Our mission is to eliminate it — delivering compliance, CFO leadership and M&A advisory from a single, credentialed team that knows your business completely.

  • Consolidate your advisors into one trusted collective
  • CA + CPA + EA — 25+ years of combined practice experience
  • Nationwide reach, remote-first, cloud-native delivery

Common Questions

Questions we hear —
and our answers.

How do I know my clients will be properly cared for?
+
Clients are assigned to a dedicated CA/CPA — not a call centre. They receive the same quality of service, often with broader advisory capability through our Fractional CFO team. Your personal endorsement in the introduction carries the relationship through the transition.
How is a succession arrangement structured?
+
Every arrangement is structured around the practitioner's specific situation, timeline and goals. We discuss the details confidentially — reach out to start a no-obligation conversation and we will walk through how it works for your specific practice.
Can I stay involved part-time after the transition?
+
Entirely your choice. Many partners remain as senior advisors for specific long-term clients. The structure accommodates your preference — some are fully hands-off within months, others check in regularly for years. There is no fixed requirement either way.
Do you work with brokers representing practices?
+
Yes — we work directly with brokers and advisors representing retirement-minded practitioners. If you are representing a practice and want to explore whether our model is a fit, contact us directly for a confidential discussion of the opportunity.
How does white-label overflow support work for active firms?
+
We operate under your brand, your SLA, inside your existing software. Extensions, overflow returns, bookkeeping clients — we handle preparation and delivery seamlessly. Per-return pricing or monthly retainer arrangements are available depending on volume and scope.
Are you equipped to serve clients across all 50 states?
+
Yes. We are fully remote and cloud-native — operating in QBO, Xero, Drake and other platforms your clients already use. Our CA/CPA team handles federal and all state filings. Geography is not a constraint for any of our service lines.
